Who are we

 
 

BlueWave is dedicated to educating, advising, and providing the
tools that aid in the day-to-day life of a professional.

 

The business technology marketplace is diverse, and most buyers struggle to determine which options are best for them. At BlueWave, we don’t think this should be such a challenge. That’s why our team is dedicated to fulfilling our clients’ demands. We help buyers make well-informed purchase decisions through comprehensive product listings, industry analysis and user-generated reviews. In addition to serving buyers, we work with vendors to grow their customer base through our unique demand generation programs. These programs improve product awareness by placing matched solutions in front of their ideal customers.

Our company is headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya and was named to the Inc. 5000 list of Africa’s Fastest-Growing Private Companies for the fourth year in a row in 2018.
